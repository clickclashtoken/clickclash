Electrical expertise
you can trust.

Lasini Electrical (Pty) Ltd is a licensed electrical contractor delivering professional services to residential, commercial, and industrial clients across South Africa — with deep roots and extensive experience in the Eastern Cape, including East London and surrounds.

Every job we take on is completed to SANS 10142 standards. We don't cut corners, we don't outsource quality, and we don't leave a site until the work is right. Our clients come back — and they refer their neighbours.

What We Do

The full spectrum of
electrical contracting.

From a single COC to a full industrial MV installation — we handle it.

Certificate of Compliance (COC)

Residential and commercial COC issuance — legally required for property transfers and insurance. Fast, thorough, accurate.

Electrical Safety Inspections

Comprehensive installation inspections identifying non-compliance, hazards and risks before they become costly problems.

Pre-Sale Property Inspections

Preparing your property for sale? We identify and resolve electrical issues before your estate agent does.

Fault Compliance Corrections

Failed an inspection? We identify and fix every non-conformity, then re-certify — one team, start to finish.

House Wiring & Rewiring

New builds or full rewires — neat, code-compliant wiring that lasts and passes COC first time.

DB Board Upgrades

Old or overloaded distribution boards replaced with safe, modern units — including surge protection and RCBO fitting.

Lighting Installation

Indoor and outdoor lighting — downlights, pendants, security lights, garden lighting and LED conversions.

Geyser, Stove & Appliances

Safe, code-compliant connections for geysers, stoves and high-draw appliances — with correct earth bonding.

Fault Finding & Power Failures

Systematic diagnostics to locate the exact fault — fast. No guesswork, no unnecessary part replacements.

Plugs, Switches & Sockets

Replacements, additions and relocations — including USB outlets and 3-phase sockets where required.

Office & Retail Installations

Complete electrical fit-outs for offices, shops and mixed-use spaces — lighting, power and data containment.

Shop Fitting Electrical

Electrical works coordinated with shop fitters — track lighting, feature lighting, POS power and signage circuits.

LED Lighting Upgrades

Convert fluorescent and halogen systems to energy-efficient LED — reduce your electricity bill immediately.

Power Distribution Setup

Subboards, busbar chambers and distribution for multi-tenant buildings and high-demand commercial premises.

Emergency Repair Services

Electrical faults that shut down business get priority response. We diagnose and restore power fast.

Maintenance Contracts

Ongoing electrical maintenance agreements — scheduled inspections, testing and reactive repair on call.

MV Electrical Installations

Medium-voltage switchgear, transformers, ring main units and HV cabling installed to SANS standards by qualified personnel.

LV Electrical Installations

Low-voltage distribution, busbar systems, cable containment and all associated LV works for new and existing plant.

Industrial Wiring Systems

Armoured cabling, cable tray systems, conduit and trunking for manufacturing plants and industrial buildings.

Machinery & Equipment Wiring

Control panels, motor starters, VSD wiring and PLC connections for production equipment of any complexity.

Power Distribution Systems

High-current busbars, transformer LV boards and distribution systems for high-demand industrial operations.

Industrial Fault Diagnostics

Thermal imaging, insulation testing and systematic fault-finding on industrial electrical systems — minimum downtime.

Preventative Maintenance

Scheduled checks, thermography scans, torque checks and safety audits — keeping faults from becoming failures.

24/7 Emergency Repairs

Electrical faults don't keep office hours. Neither do we. Call us anytime — we'll be there with the right equipment.

System Upgrades

Ageing infrastructure upgraded to current standards — DB boards, cabling, earthing and surge protection.

Overload & Tripping Resolution

Persistent tripping analysed and resolved properly — not just reset. Load calculations, circuit balancing and protection sizing.
